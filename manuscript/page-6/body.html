Personal life

Arvind is born in Krittika Nakshatra, which is materialistic in nature with Spear as its symbol. This Nakshatra has all the qualities of the royals and has blessed him with comforts of a great and supportive family. Sun rules over his Krittika Nakshatra indicating a kingly life with Raj Guna, i.e path of pursuits and happiness.

Since his Nakshatras have Spear as its symbol, it also shows his upright and straightforward nature. Arvind Kejriwal will be critical of his political peers throughout his life and will find this quality of his among the most useful tools in his career to find real gems from the fake ones.

On the personal front Arvind Kejriwal is blessed with smart and intelligent children who will cultivate the same ideals as their father and will bear his torch of honesty and helping the poor and destitute.
