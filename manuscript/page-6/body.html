Personal Life

He is born in Krittika Nakshatra, which is materialistic in nature with Spear, as its symbol. As this Nakshatra has all the luxury qualities, it blessed him with all the Royal comforts. Sun's rulership over the Krittika Nakshatra with Kingly qualities fills him with Raj Guna which indicates a desire for expansion of pursuits.

As this Nakshatra has Spear as its symbol, it shows his straightforward, direct, piercing and Razor sharp thought process. Criticism and fault finding becomes the most prominent quality of the person born in this Nakshatra.
